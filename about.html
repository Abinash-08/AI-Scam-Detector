About the Project

Every year, thousands of students and citizens are cheated by fake scholarships, scam internships, and bogus government scheme portals. These websites steal personal data, charge fake registration fees, and erode trust in genuine platforms.

Our solution, AIShield, is an AI-assisted scam detection system that combines URL reputation checks, content analysis, and rule-based verification to estimate how risky an opportunity link might be.

What We Do

  • Analyze domain structure, HTTPS usage, and risky TLDs.
  • Detect scammy language like “pay registration fee” or “no eligibility – 100% guaranteed”.
  • Cross-check against a small curated dataset of verified and known scam portals.
  • Generate a Scam Score (0–100) with a simple traffic-light verdict.

Hackathon Implementation

For this hackathon demo, we simulate the AI/NLP and database parts on the front-end using:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript-based dashboard
  • Rule-based heuristics for scam detection
  • A sample JSON dataset of known genuine and fake domains
  • Expandable design for Flask/FastAPI + real NLP later
